# 523 - Exam 1 Notes

- Terminologies
  - OSI Reference Model
  - dB = 10 * log2(s/n)
  - S/N = 10^dB/10
  - $$dB=10*log_{10}(\frac{S}{N})$$
  - $$\frac{S}{N}=10^{\frac{dB}{10}}$$
- Nyquist's Theorem
  - Bitrate = 2f * log2(b)
  - $$Bitrate=2f*log_{2}(B)$$
- Shannon's Noisy Theorem
  - Bitrate = Bandwidth * log2(1 + s/n)
  - $$Bitrate=Bandwidth*log_{2}(1+\frac{S}{N}$$
- Baseband Transmission
  - NRZ
    - 0: High
    - 1: Low
  - NRZ-I
    - 0: Stay
    - 1: Flip sides
  - Manchester
    - 0: Top-mid-bottom
    - 1: bottom-mid-top
  - Differential Manchester
    - 0: Flip, mid, return
    - 1: mid, flip, stay
  - Bipolar/AMI
    - 0: middle
    - 1: flip opposite from previous
- Passband Transmission
  - AM/ASK
    - 0: Level 1 up/down
    - 1: Level 2 up/down
  - FM/FSK
    - 0: Level 1, 1 up/down
    - 1: Level 1, 2 up/down
  - PM/PSK
    - 0: Level 1, V shape
    - 1: Level 2, up/down
  - DPSK
  - QAM
- Analog to Digital Conversion
  - PAM
  - PCM
  - DPCM
  - Quantization Error
- Transmission Media
- Multiplexing Techniques
  - FDM
  - TDM (STDM, ATDM)
  - CDMA
  - WDM
- Switching Techniques
  - Circuit Switching
  - Message Switching
